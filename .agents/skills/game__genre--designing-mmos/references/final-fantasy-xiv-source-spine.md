# Final Fantasy XIV Source Spine

Read this when making factual or historical claims about FFXIV’s recovery, player-count milestones, free trial, Endwalker congestion, Duty Support, Adventurer Plates, Community Finder, or the 2021 WoW-to-FFXIV exodus discourse.

Keep facts, support level, and design interpretation separate.

## Claim Discipline

### Safe claims

- FFXIV is a major modern MMORPG with over 30 million total registered accounts, including free-trial accounts.
- FFXIV’s free trial is unusually generous in content scope and time limit.
- FFXIV added Duty Support to allow MSQ dungeons with NPCs, reducing mandatory grouping friction.
- FFXIV has official identity/social surfaces such as Adventurer Plates and Community Finder.
- Endwalker demand caused major congestion and the operator restricted sales/free-trial intake temporarily.
- Blizzard MAUs fell substantially between the 2017 record and 2021, but those MAUs are Blizzard-wide, not WoW-only.
- The 2021 WoW-to-FFXIV exodus was a real discourse/cultural event involving streamers and player migration, but it was not universal and not cleanly measurable from public data.

### Unsafe / overclaimed statements

- “Everyone left WoW for FFXIV.”
- “Asmongold caused FFXIV’s success.”
- “WoW had 46 million players in 2017 and fell to 26 million in 2021.”
- “FFXIV surpassed WoW in subscribers.”
- “Dungeon Finder killed WoW and FFXIV won because it is friendlier.”
- “FFXIV has no decline risks.”
- “Free trial caused the exodus.”
- “Yoshida told players to quit.” Better: FFXIV’s leadership has often been associated with healthy break-taking and returnability rhetoric; verify the exact quote before using it.

## Design Interpretation

The useful design interpretation is:

```text
WoW weakening created a search moment.
Asmongold and other streamers made the search visible.
FFXIV could receive the wave because it had already built trust, story reputation, onboarding runway, low-pressure grouping, social expression, and patch anticipation.
Endwalker congestion then tested operator trust, and Square Enix’s response became part of the social narrative.
```

The wrong interpretation is:

```text
FFXIV won because WoW lost.
```

The better interpretation is:

```text
A durable MMO wins exodus moments before they happen.
```

## Verification Notes

When using this spine in external-facing work, verify exact dates and source language. Prefer official sources for player-count milestones, patch features, and financial metrics. Use press/game journalism for cultural events such as the Asmongold wave, while labeling them as discourse/culture evidence rather than population proof.
